Extra Curricular

Here at Peak, we believe extra curricular will help your child exeed in talents other than academics. We have 4 main specials, art, music, PE (physical education/exercise), technology, starting at Kindergarten to 5th grade, one special each quarter of the school year. After this, we use electives which allows students to choose certain activities within, art, music, PE, music. One requirement is you have to choose at least one activity for PE.


If you have any questions, feel free to email us or ask us in person. Thanks for reading. :)

Classes

Classes are different within different classrooms and grades. If we include Social Studies, we have 4 classes; ELA (English Language Arts), Math, Science, and Social Studies. Three classes if we don't include social studies. Teachers will go more in detail in classrooms about schedules, amount of classes, how long they will be, ect.

Lunch, recess, and spcecials timings vary from grade levels. Then dismissal, which is when the students leave school. Hope this helps :)
